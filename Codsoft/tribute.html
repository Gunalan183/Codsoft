About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, investor, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He is known for his vision, integrity, and commitment to social causes.

Throughout his career, Ratan Tata has led Tata Group with exceptional leadership, steering it through various challenges while fostering innovation and sustainability.
